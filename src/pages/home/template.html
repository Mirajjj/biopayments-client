Our Future Vision!

Through centuries money exchange process has been consistently and unavoidably evolving by a search for more comfortable, secure and faster way to transfer money from one party to another.

Nowadays we see how gracefully digital payments are integrated in our daily life. Almost everybody has a bank account and either credit or debit cards. In recent years appeared virtual currency, mobile payments. Humanity has developed enough to step into a new phase of delivering payment experience. It is time for itemless and cardless biometric payments!

A decade credit card served as a role of digital wallet, as a thread or a link to a user's bank account. While crads helped to get rid of having massive cash amount in the pocket it is still required to have something to represent user's connection to user's cash flow. In same time technology has been moving forward spreading an innovations through out the world. Biometric information became an essentail part in many industrial and administrational fields. It steadly becomes a unique identification process securely and definitelly identifing a person.

We have a strong belief that in the nearest future people won't need to have a purse or credit cards as their body will be a secure direct link to their bank accounts. Payment processes will be simplified for a user, delivering best possible experince.
